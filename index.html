About Me

Clarity, momentum,
outcomes.

Hello! I'm Steve Hering and I live in the Columbus, Ohio area with my wife and our two boys. By day I'm a Senior Consultant, Experience Design at Slalom — partnering with hospital systems, health insurance companies, and logistics organizations to illuminate new paths to solving their toughest challenges.

My work sits at the intersection of business, design, and technology — leading discovery, journey and empathy mapping, product strategy, and translating ambiguity into experiences teams can actually deliver. Outside of work you'll find me with my family, playing board games, drawing, and thinking about how to spend just a little more time cooking.

How I Work

My Design Process

Every great product starts with a question. I follow a structured yet flexible process that keeps humans at the center of every decision.

01

Discover

Research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to define the real problem.

02

Define

Synthesis, personas, and journey maps to focus on what matters most.

03

Ideate

Sketching, workshops, and divergent thinking to explore the solution space.

04

Prototype

Wireframes and interactive prototypes — from lo-fi to pixel-perfect.

05

Test & Ship

Usability testing, iteration, and hand-off with clear documentation.
